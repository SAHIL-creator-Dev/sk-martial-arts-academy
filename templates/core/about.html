About the Dojo

Karate for all.

S.K Martial Arts Academy operates under the Renbukai Karate-Do Federation India, affiliated with All Japan Renbukai International, and is a member of the Japan Karate Federation and World Karate Federation. The academy is recognised by the Indian Olympic Association, the International Olympic Committee, and the Ministry of Sports & Youth Affairs, Govt. of India.
